Tech Background

My computer interests all started my sophomore year of high school. I had always been a tech kid, but I wanted to start a project that challenged my knowledge and allowed for more future opportunities. This was when I decided to build my own custom PC. I researched what I needed to do and ended up building the PC that I still use to this day. I enjoyed this project so much that I offered to build my friends PC's as well, all they had to do was buy the parts. I ended up building 3 of my friends computers over the next year. I then wanted to learn more about how games and applications work and this led me to my first programming classes in high school. These classes led me to become a Computer Science major. Once I started learning more in my classes at St. Thomas, it expanded exponentially. I am always on the look out for new topics to learn and ways to perfect my skills. I have never had a doubt in my mind about what I wanted to do for a career because I know this is what I love to do.


Education
